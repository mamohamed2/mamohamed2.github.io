EAYBL Teams and Players

Davion

  • Strength: vision, ball handling, high IQ player makes teammates better
  • Weakness: shooting, too unselfish at times
  • Comparison: Rajon Rondo

Ahmed Bahsane

  • Strength: handle, 3 level scoring, lightning quickness. Ability to take over a game
  • Weakness: assertiveness, size
  • Comparison: Kemba Walker

Kobe Musse

  • Strength: midrange specialist. Floater. Can take over game with his energy. Game changer when locked in
  • Weakness: prefers individual success over team success. Can be too ball dominant.
  • Comparison: Andre Miller

Liban Haye

  • Strength: shooting, ball handling, play making.
  • Weakness: speed, effort, turnovers.
  • Comparison: Javale McGee

Munir

  • Strength: shooting, ball handling, play making.
  • Weakness: speed, effort, turnovers.
  • Comparison: Evan Fournier

Don C

  • Strength: Consistent Shooter with range, excellent help defender great instincts to alter shots.
  • Weakness: lacks speed to make impact on ball defense. Playmaking
  • Comparison: JaMychal Green

Abdullahi M

  • Strength: excellent scorer and ball handler with great touch around the rim. Creates shot on all defenders.
  • Weakness: facilitating, production off ball.
  • Comparison: Lou Williams

Abdullahi Issa

  • Strengths: Excellent shooter with ability to catch fire from deep. Strength and size to finish through any contact.
  • Weaknesses: Facilitating, effort, athleticism
  • Comparison: Buddy Hield

Abdimalik

  • Strengths: Defensive minded guard with ability to shoot from 3. Do-it-all kind of player.
  • Weaknesses: Defense, speed
  • Comparison: Duncan Robinson

Abdirahman 1Take

  • Strengths: excellent shooter from the perimeter. Crafty finisher around rim.
  • Weaknesses: Ball handling/Shot selection
  • Comparison: Terry Rozier

Turjuman

  • Strengths: athletic guard. Physical on the glass and finishes thru contact.
  • Weaknesses: shooting, decision making, temper
  • Comparison: Jerami Grant

Kyro

  • Strengths: lights out shooter with unlimited range. High energy player who can catch fire quick.
  • Weaknesses: playmaking, defense
  • Comparison: Jason Terry

Andy

  • Strengths: lock down defender who can guard multiple positions. Strong enough to punish defenders in post.
  • Weaknesses: Shooting ,decision making
  • Comparison: Jason Terry

Noona

  • Strengths: High IQ player versatile. Floor general.
  • Weaknesses: Stamina, ball handling, 3pt shooting
  • Comparison: Nikola Jokic

Abdullahi KD

  • Strengths: Scoring on all levels. Height and skill set make him one of the toughest players to guard. Rebounding
  • Weaknesses: Defense, not assertive enough.
  • Comparison: Andrew Wiggins

Abdikarim (Fulan)

  • Strengths: Rebounding. Constantly active on the glass. Freak athletcism, can finish on any defender. Unstoppable when going downhill
  • Weaknesses: Defense, ball handling/decision making.
  • Comparison: Montrezl Harrell

Kamal Mursal

  • Strengths: shooting, slashing. Crafty finisher with great potential.
  • Weaknesses: Confidence, puts individual success over team success. Lacks speed
  • Comparison: Jayson Tatum

Hanad

  • Strengths: All around talent, scoring/rebounding/handle. Fills the stat sheet.
  • Weaknesses: Decision making, defense
  • Comparison: Lamar Odom

Mohamed Ali

  • Strengths: insane handles, layup maestro. Great team player. Clutch.
  • Weaknesses: Can go long stretches in a game without impact.
  • Comparison: Joe Johnson

Ismail Bahsane

  • Strengths: Speed/Aggression. Lock down defender who can go coast to coast. Effort/energy
  • Weaknesses: Erratic at times, not a team player at times. Right hand.
  • Comparison: Russell Westbrook

Harun Ahmed

  • Strengths: Shooting, leadership. ability to hit big shots
  • Weaknesses: lacks speed, can go stretches without an impact.
  • Comparison: Dion Waiters

Harun Ahmed

  • Comparison: Dion Waiters

Abdul (Big Drip)

  • Strengths: Lengthy, excellent 3&D player, can catch fire quick and fill stat sheet
  • Weaknesses: can go stretches without impacting the game. Shot selection.
  • Comparison: Nicolas Batum

Ismail (Dripish)

  • Strengths: Lights-out shooter, smooth finisher around rim.
  • Weaknesses: Defense, playmaking
  • Comparison: Joe Harris

Hashim (The Dream)

  • Strengths: Lights out shooter, corner specialist. Great teammate
  • Weaknesses: Lacks size to guard bigger players.
  • Comparison: JJ Redick

Alawi
